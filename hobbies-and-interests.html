# Talents

# Drawing

I can safely say that I was born with a natural drawing talent, since my childhood I always loved to draw, I wanted to be a painter. But, like it happens to a lot of us, I changed my mind once I was getting older.

I had a little artist career that lasted like 2-3 years between 2008 and 2011. It started when I was 13 years old when I won a drawing contest in high school and as a reward I got a scholarship in a little art school, then I got a scholarship from a larger organization called ICBC (opens new window) where I learned and improved a lot.

The art life and school were constantly colliding so I decided to cut out the art life and focus on school, since I was learning programming and it was time consuming so I didn't have much time left for art.
